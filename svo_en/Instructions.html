Instructions

In this short experiment, you were randomly assigned to another participant. You and the other person remain anonymous. All your decisions remain confidential.

You'll make a total of six decisions in which you'll determine the distribution of points between you and the other person. In each case, you'll have a choice between 10 different options, which differ in terms of the points that you and the other person will each get. The options are as follows:

100 98 96 94 93 91 89 87 85 For you
50 54 59 63 68 72 76 81 85 For other person

In the experiment, there are checkboxes 1-9 below each of which you choose one. Your payout depends on the points you collect yourself and the points the other person assigns to you. Points are converted to GBP at a fixed rate of 1:1000.

There are no right or wrong answers. It's all about your personal assessments.
